**Audience Analysis Report**  
Up to 1,000 XP, due February 15

In this assignment, you will analyze how the same business information needs to be communicated differently to three distinct audiences within an organization, based on your team’s scenario from “[The Mixed Message](https://engl-100wb-s25.github.io/TheMixedMessage/)” (Thursday, January 29). You can refine your understanding of specific forms of writing for these audiences through “[Genre Quest](https://engl-100wb-s25.github.io/GenreQuest/)” (Thursday, February 5). Although you and your team members will base your reports on the same in-class scenario, this is an individual writing assignment. This exercise demonstrates the importance of audience adaptation in professional communication and develops your strategic thinking about stakeholder needs.

# **Learning Objectives:**

* Demonstrate understanding of audience analysis principles  
* Apply rhetorical strategies to professional communication  
* Practice adapting content for different stakeholders  
* Develop professional document design skills

This assignment supports the following Course Learning Outcomes:

1\. Apply rhetorical principles to analyze and adapt communication for diverse business audiences, purposes, and contexts.  
2\. Produce professional-quality documents in various business genres, demonstrating mastery of conventions and best practices.

---

# **Assignment Components:**

1. Executive Summary (200-300 words)  
* Clear statement of purpose (describe your scenario\!)  
* Summary of recommendations  
* If needed, you can write this after our first-draft workshop on Thursday, February 12, since this is your first time writing an Executive Summary\!  
2. Audience Profiles (400-500 words total; based in “The Mixed Message”)  
   For each of three audiences, analyze (about 150 words each):  
* Technical knowledge level  
* Primary concerns and priorities  
* Preferred communication channels  
* Decision-making authority  
* Time constraints and preferences  
3. Communication Strategy (250-350 words; based in “Genre Quest”)  
* Specific recommendations for message adaptation  
* Channel selection rationale  
* Timing considerations  
4. Sample Excerpts (100-300 words each)  
* Demonstrate the same key information written three ways for your different audiences

# **Format Requirements:**

* Professional business formatting  
* Clear headings and organization  
  * You can use [APA style headings](https://apastyle.apa.org/style-grammar-guidelines/paper-format/headings) as a default\! 🙂  
* Appropriate spacing and font choices  
* Citations if external sources are used
